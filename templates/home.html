FindVaccines Canada

A tool for quickly finding a vaccine in Canada

Our tool helps you find nearby vaccines

We use data collection software to aggregate available appointments near you. Our goal is to make getting a vaccine in Canada as simple as possible.

Canada can do better at deploying doses quickly when they're received from manufacturers. Getting vaccinated as soon as you're eligible saves lives, and we want to make that as easy as possible.

What VaccineFinder can do:

  • Help you find a vaccine as quickly as possible if you're eligible
  • Direct you towards appointment booking platforms
  • Direct you to info about eligibility

What VaccineFinder cannot do:

  • Give you medical advice
  • Sign you up for a vaccine appointment
